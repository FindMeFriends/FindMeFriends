About Us

About us_

Welcome to FindMeFriends! Our mission is to make connecting with new people easier and more spontaneous, wherever you are. We understand how challenging it can be to make friends in a new place, so we've created a mobile app that brings people together through live location sharing, an in-app messaging function, and many other features. With FindMeFriends, you can find nearby users, check out their profiles, and take the first steps towards a new friendship. Just open the app, see who's around, and make real-world connections in real time.
